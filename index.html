UPL NORTHERN GROWER REWARDS PROGRAM - 2020

UPL NA Inc. is excited to offer growers in the northern states a cash-back program of crop protection solutions for multiple crops. Growers can earn up to $3.00/acre when they purchase qualifying UPL products between September 1, 2019 and August 31, 2020.

QUALIFIYING PURCHASE DATES:

Product purchases must be made between Sept 1, 2019 and August 31, 2020.

INCENTIVE VALUE:

EARN UP TO $3.00 PER ACRE!
This is a cash rebate offer paid to grower upon submission of all proof of purchase documents in accordance with the terms and conditions below. This is not a points or merchandise incentive.

ELIGIBLE GEOGRAPHY:

ID, MN, MT, ND, SD, WA and WI

PROGRAM CONDITIONS
1.To qualify for the cash-back incentive, all 2020 participating brands net purchases must be reported to TKXS, UPL’s data collection vendor, by the Retailer where the participating brands were purchased by August 31, 2020.
2.Earned cash-back incentives will be paid to the Grower based on the net sales transactions as of August 31, 2020 reported by the Retailer. If the Retailer has authorized the return of any participating brand, for any reason, prior to August 31, 2020, Grower data of reported participating brand purchases will be reduced and may bring purchases below the required quantities and reduce or disqualify Grower from earning a cash-back incentive under the Program.
3.UPL is in no way liable or responsible for any Grower’s failure to pay the Retailer.
4.Payment of cash-back incentives will be paid to the Grower or entity identified as the purchaser via Retailer submission to TKXS.
5.No hand-written proof of purchase documents will be accepted without prior written approval from UPL. Proof of purchase invoice(s) must include the UPL Authorized Retailer’s name and address, Grower name and address, product name, volume, purchase date and invoice number.
6.Individual Growers may not aggregate purchases in order to qualify for a cash-back incentive under this Program.
7.UPL reserves the right to audit all Program claims related to Retailer and Grower reported information and reserves the right of final decision on any cash-back incentives to the Grower. All payments are subject to final approval by UPL.
8.Sales to other Retailers and/or to Distributors, or through on-line product exchanges or auctions, co-ops, buying groups, or to end-users that the Authorized Retailer knows, or has reason to know, intends to resell product, will not qualify for payments under this Program.
9.If a grower is participating in the Seed grower program and the Northern Grower Rewards Program, UPL will pay out against the Seed Program first and then the balance of what is earned against the Northern Grower Rewards Program.
10.UPL reserves the right, in its discretion, to modify (including changing the date when an incentive will be paid), suspend or terminate this Program at any time without prior notice.

To find out more about the 2020 UPL Northern Grower Rewards Program, talk to your retailer or UPL representative.
